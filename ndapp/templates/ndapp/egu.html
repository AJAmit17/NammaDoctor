Emergency Information

Call Emergency Services

If you are facing a life-threatening situation, call your local emergency services immediately. In most places, this is 911.

Provide clear and concise information to the operator, including your location and the nature of the emergency.

First Aid Guidelines

Knowing basic first aid techniques can save lives. Here are a few key guidelines:

  • CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation): Learn how to perform CPR on adults, children, and infants.
  • Choking: Understand the Heimlich maneuver for choking victims of different ages.
  • Bleeding: Apply direct pressure to stop bleeding. Use clean cloth or bandages.
  • Burns: Cool burns with cold water and cover them with a sterile bandage or cloth.

Remember, these guidelines are for immediate assistance until professional help arrives.

Virtual Health Services

In some cases, you might be able to receive virtual emergency consultations. If available, use our virtual health service to consult with a medical professional quickly.

However, virtual services should not replace in-person medical care for severe emergencies.
